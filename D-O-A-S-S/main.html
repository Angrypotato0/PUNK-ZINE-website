Introduction
This is the story of how I tried to quit smoking weed.

In my 20s I used to say "I will quit when I'm 30". Now, I'm 30 and
I'm still as much of a stoner as in my younger years.
I got so used to smoking every day and I can feel how my body is
slowly paying the price for it... my nose is permanently stuffed, I
have constant sneeze attacks and when I get a cold it knocks my lungs
out for a week.
It's time to change.

This website is a manifest of my thoughts and feelings during this
turbulent time in my life.
